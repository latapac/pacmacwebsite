Model

Mac 150

Double Head Tube Filler.

Tube Filler for when reliable production is your goal. Production over 60,000 tubes 8 hours with a rated speed of 150 Tubes / Minute and handling complete variety of tubes. Combinational Beam available capable of closing both Laminated/Plastic and Aluminium Tubes.

Mac 240

Four Head Tube Filler.

Tube Filler for high speed packaging requirement. Production over 100,000 tubes 8 hours with a rated speed of 240 Tubes / Minute and handling complete variety of tubes. Available in Hot Air & Metal Folding Variant.

Mac 75

Single Head Tube Filler.

Tube Filler with a compact footprint allowing quick change overs. Ideal for small batch sizes, production upto 30,000 tubes in 8 hours with a rated speed of 75 Tubes / Minute and handling complete variety of tubes. Combinational Beam available capable of closing both Laminated/Plastic and Aluminium Tubes.

Company
